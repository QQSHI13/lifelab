Rule Sets
Conway's Life (B3/S23)
The classic cellular automaton. Cells are born with 3 neighbors and survive with 2 or 3. Most patterns work with this rule.
HighLife (B36/S23)
Like Conway's but cells also born with 6 neighbors. Famous for the Replicator pattern that copies itself!
Seeds (B2/S)
Explosive rule where cells are born with 2 neighbors but never survive. Creates expanding patterns.
Day & Night (B3678/S34678)
Symmetric rule where dead and alive cells behave similarly. Supports complex spaceships.
